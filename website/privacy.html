Privacy Policy

PromoKit keeps your campaign data under your control.

Last updated: May 16, 2026

Data You Add

PromoKit stores the app names, product names, offer-code CSV contents, recipient names, sharing status, and expiry dates that you choose to import or enter in the app.

Where Data Is Stored

PromoKit stores campaign state on your device. When iCloud Documents is available for the app, PromoKit uses iCloud Documents so your data can follow your signed-in Apple account. If iCloud is unavailable, PromoKit stores the data locally in Application Support.

Network Requests

PromoKit can look up public App Store information, such as app name, bundle ID, App Store URL, and artwork, when you provide an App Store URL or app ID. Those lookups use Apple's public App Store services.

Tracking and Advertising

PromoKit does not include advertising, third-party analytics, or third-party tracking SDKs.

Sharing and Exporting

PromoKit only shares or exports campaign data when you use a share, copy, QR card, or CSV export action. Those actions use the destinations you choose through iOS.

Contact

For questions about this policy, use the support options listed on the support page.

PromoKit is free, open source, and built for App Store developers.
